Minisymposium Proposal Submission

General Information

A minisymposium consists of one or a maximum of two sessions of coordinated presentations. Each session should include four presentations, with each presentation lasting approximately 25-30 minutes (including discussion time).

Submission Requirements

To submit a minisymposium proposal, organizers must provide the following information:

  • Title of the minisymposium
  • Short description (max 500 words) outlining the scope and objectives
  • Name and affiliation of the organizer(s)
  • List of speakers with their names and affiliations
