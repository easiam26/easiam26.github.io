About the Conference

The 19th East Asia SIAM (EASIAM) Annual Meeting brings together researchers, educators, and students in the field of industrial and applied mathematics from East and Southeast Asia. The conference provides a forum for the exchange of ideas and the presentation of recent results in all areas of applied mathematics.

Jeju Island, a UNESCO World Heritage site, offers a stunning backdrop for this year's meeting, fostering both academic excellence and cultural exchange.
